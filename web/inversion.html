Artifact > creator inversion

For 11 verified (creator, artifact) pairs, the chart shows where each lands on the NameRank axis and the conditional-probe attribution flow (C → A = fraction of responses about the creator that mention the artifact; A → C = reverse). In 8 of 11 pairs, the artifact outranks its creator (blue rows). For solo creators of single named artifacts (Datasette, nanoGPT, Tianshou, LangChain, Cursor) the inversion is uniform.

Attribution flow

Two regimes are visible: famous artifact, anonymous creator (C→A high, A→C low) — Aman Sanger/Cursor, Harrison Chase/LangChain, Aravind Srinivas/Perplexity; and paired in corpus (both high) — Lilian Weng/lilianweng.github.io, Mira Murati/Thinking Machines Lab, Tri Dao/FlashAttention. Andrej Karpathy/nanoGPT is the inverse regime: a famous-for-many-things creator whose probes rarely surface a specific artifact.

Per-response mediation on Jiayi Weng

On the Jiayi Weng probe specifically, models that mention Tianshou in their response score Jiayi at 0.71; non-mentioning models score 0.35 — a +0.36 score lift from artifact-mention. The mentioning models are exactly the frontier-reasoning subset: claude-opus-think, claude-sonnet-think, gemini-3-flash-think, gemini-3.1-pro, glm-5.1-think, gpt-5.5-think, kimi-k2.6-think, plus one Gemma variant.
